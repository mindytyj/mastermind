Click on the Check button once your guess has been selected.


If your selection includes the correct colour peg in the correct position, the side peg will reflect red.


If your selection includes the correct colour peg in the wrong position, the side peg will reflect black.


If your selection is not in Masterio's secret code, the side peg will remain white.
